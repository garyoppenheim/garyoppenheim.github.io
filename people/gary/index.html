Research summary

I'm a Lecturer in the School of Psychology at Bangor University, General Editor for the Cambridge University Press journal, Language and Cognition, and head of the Language Production Lab. I use experimental psychology and computational modelling to understand how the mind works, with particular focus on processes and representations underlying language production. But I'm more generally interested in language, learning, and memory, and lots of other things that spider off from one or more of those.
